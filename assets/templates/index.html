How It Works

From raw signals
to actionable insight

Our pipeline captures biosignals through wearable sensors, extracts meaningful features, and applies trained classification models to predict your stress state in seconds.

01
Wear the Sensor

Attach a wrist or chest wearable device. Sensors continuously record physiological signals including ECG, EDA, temperature and more.

02
Signal Processing

Raw sensor streams are cleaned, segmented and processed into statistical, time-domain and frequency-domain features per signal window.

03
Model Inference

Trained ML and deep learning models — RNN, XGBoost, Random Forest and more — classify the current affective state with high accuracy.

04
View Results

Receive a clear, real-time classification — Baseline, Stress, Amusement or Meditation — with confidence scores and trend history.

Multimodal Sensing

Seven physiological
signal streams

Data is captured simultaneously from RespiBAN (chest) and Empatica E4 (wrist) sensors, providing a rich, complementary view of your body's stress response.

{% for signal in signals_list %} {% endfor %}
ECG
Electrocardiogram

Measures electrical heart activity. HRV and HR extracted for stress markers.

EDA
Electrodermal Activity

Skin conductance rises with stress due to increased sweat gland activity.

BVP
Blood Volume Pulse

Optical PPG signal captures peripheral blood flow and heart rate variation.

TEMP
Body Temperature

Peripheral skin temperature fluctuations correlate with autonomic arousal.

RESP
Respiration

Breathing rate and depth shift measurably under cognitive and emotional stress.
